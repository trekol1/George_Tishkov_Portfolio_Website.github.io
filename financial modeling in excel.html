Financial modeling
in Excel

Building a financial model to analyse different scenarios of compsnie's further expansion.

I was working in a real estate agency, which was actively expanding. My challange was to build a model, analysing different scenarios of companie's further expansion.
The model had to consider different assumptions and showcase possible outcomes depending on a specific scenario.

Main assumptions I had to work on:

  • Agents should be devided into 3 groups by ammount of deals they process monthly
  • 2 options of getting the initial funding: bank loan and attracting a private investor, and compare both scenarios refering to repayment rates
  • 2 possible taxing models (taxes are calculated from gross revenue by rate of 6%, or from profit by rate of 15%)
  • Adjustable agency's fee
  • Adjustable fixed costs such as rent, salaries etc., and adjustable variable costs which depends on the amount of agents

For each assumption and scenario the model should calculate all the cashflows devided by type and detailed monthly.

The data was messed up and only basic features are available for the demonstration, but major types of calculations and techniques I used are shown.
